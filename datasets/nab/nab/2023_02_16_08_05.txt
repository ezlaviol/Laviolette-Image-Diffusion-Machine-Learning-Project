2023-02-16T08:05:00+11:00 : Feb 16 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  on Thursday reported a 19% jump in its first-quarter cash profit, benefiting from the central bank's fast-paced monetary policy tightening and business lending volume growth. 	The country's second-biggest lender posted cash earnings of A$2.15 billion ($1.48 billion) for the quarter ended Dec. 31, compared with A$1.80 billion a year ago.
