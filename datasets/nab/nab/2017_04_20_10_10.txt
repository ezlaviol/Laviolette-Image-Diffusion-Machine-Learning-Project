2017-04-20T10:10:00+10:00 : April 20 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)   Nab has elected to redeem all outstanding nsn in  accordance with their terms  NSN will be redeemed on monday, 19 june 2017, being their  first optional redemption date  NSN were issued by nab in 2012 and trade on asx under code  'nabhb'  For each NSN held nsn holders will receive a cash payment  of $100 plus $1.1331 interest  $633,692,300 of nsn (representing 6,336,923 nsn at $100  each) is currently outstanding.
