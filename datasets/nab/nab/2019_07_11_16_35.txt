2019-07-11T16:35:00+10:00 : SYDNEY, July 11 (Reuters) - Australia's biggest banks said on Thursday that a telecommunications outage had knocked out some teller machines and branch services. 	Westpac Banking Corporation  (WBC)  said on Twitter that some automatic teller machines in Sydney were not operating due to a network issue, and Commonwealth Bank of Australia  (CBA)  said some of its machines were having issues. 	The country's biggest telecom provider, Telstra Corp Ltd  (TLS) , said it was investigating the issue and that a number of large customers were affected nationwide.
