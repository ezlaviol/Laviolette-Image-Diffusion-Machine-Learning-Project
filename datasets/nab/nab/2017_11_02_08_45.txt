2017-11-02T08:45:00+11:00 : Nov 2 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank (NAB) (NAB) posted a 2.5 percent rise in annual cash profit on Thursday, helped by surging home loan volumes and higher business lending margins. 	Cash profit came in at A$6.64 billion ($5.09 billion) for the year ended Sept. 30, from A$6.48 billion a year earlier, NAB said. Cash profit excludes one-offs and non-cash accounting items.  	Australia's no. 4 lender declared a final dividend of A$0.99 per share, the same as last year.	  	($1 = 1.3033 Australian dollars)
