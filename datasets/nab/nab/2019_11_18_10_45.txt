2019-11-18T10:45:00+11:00 : Nov 18 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  said on Monday it was looking to raise A$1.4 billion ($959.70 million) through the issue of medium-term notes to help beef up its capital ahead of a regulatory deadline.   	Of the total issue, A$1.18 billion of notes will carry a floating rate, and A$225 million will carry a fixed rate. Both sets of notes will be due in November 2031.		   ($1 = 1.4588 Australian dollars)
