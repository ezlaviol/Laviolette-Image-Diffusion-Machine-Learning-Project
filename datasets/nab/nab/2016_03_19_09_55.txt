2016-03-19T09:55:00+11:00 : Carey Smith of Alto Capital reckons that "NAB has finally closed the book on its disastrous UK banking adventure with the sale and spin out of Clydesdale and Yorkshire, enabling management to concentrate on its profitable Australian operations.
