2020-02-13T08:15:00+11:00 : Feb 13 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  on Thursday said cash profit in the first-quarter was unchanged, as the bank grapples with record-low interest rates.  	Australia's third-largest lender by market value posted cash earnings of A$1.65 billion ($1.11 billion) for the three months ended Dec. 31, mirroring the same figure a year ago.  	Common equity tier 1 (CET1) ratio, a closely watched measure of the bank's spare cash, rose to 10.6% at Dec. 31 from 10.38% at Sept. 30.    ($1 = 1.4843 Australian dollars)
