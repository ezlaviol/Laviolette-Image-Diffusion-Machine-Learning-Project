2020-04-07T06:46:00+10:00 : The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 1,627.46 points, or 7.73 per cent, to 22,679.99, the S&P 500 gained 175.03 points, or 7.03 per cent, to 2,663.68 and the Nasdaq Composite added 540.16 points, or 7.33 per cent, to 7,913.24.
