2022-11-09T08:05:00+11:00 : Nov 9 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB) , the country's second-largest lender, on Wednesday posted a 8.3% rise in annual cash profit as it benefited from robust home and business lending and as second-half margins were supported by rising interest rates. 	Australia's biggest business bank reported cash earnings of A$7.10 billion ($4.62 billion) for the year to September, compared with A$6.56 billion a year earlier, and analysts' estimate of A$7.08 billion according to Refinitiv Eikon. 	The bank declared a final dividend of 78 Australian cents per share, compared with 67 Australian cents apiece last year. ($1 = 1.5380 Australian dollars)
