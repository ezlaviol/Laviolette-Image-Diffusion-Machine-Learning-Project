2020-11-05T08:10:00+11:00 : Nov 5 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  on Thursday slashed its final dividend payout and posted a 36.6% fall in annual profit, hurt by charges on higher loan losses due to the COVID-19 pandemic. 	Australia's third-largest bank posted full-year cash earnings of A$3.71 billion ($2.66 billion), compared with a restated figure of A$5.85 billion last year. Analysts polled by Reuters on average expected cash earnings of A$3.82 billion. 	The company declared a final dividend of A$0.30, compared with last year's payout of A$0.83 per share.		   ($1 = 1.3937 Australian dollars)
