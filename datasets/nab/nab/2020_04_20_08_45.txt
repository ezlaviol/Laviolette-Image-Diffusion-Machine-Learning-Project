2020-04-20T08:45:00+10:00 : April 20 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  announced on Monday a number of provisions for customer remediation and changes to a software capitalisation policy that is expected to reduce its first-half earnings. 	A change to the application of NAB's software capitalisation policy will reduce earnings by A$742 million ($471.3 million)after tax, NAB said. 	Customer-related remediation charges are expected at A$268 million before tax for the period, the lender said. ($1 = 1.5743 Australian dollars)
