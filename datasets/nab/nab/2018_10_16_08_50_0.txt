2018-10-16T08:50:00+11:00 : Oct 16 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  said on Tuesday it would incur after-tax costs of A$314 million ($223.91 million) in connection with its customer remediation programme. 	The charge would reduce second-half cash earnings by about A$261 million, the bank said in a statement. 	NAB is due to report full-year earnings on November 1. ($1 = 1.4023 Australian dollars)
