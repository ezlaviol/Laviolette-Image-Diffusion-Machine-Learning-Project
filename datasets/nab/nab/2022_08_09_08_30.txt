2022-08-09T08:30:00+10:00 : Aug 9 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  on Tuesday reported an almost 6% rise in third-quarter cash profit as rising interest rates helped improve its margins. 	Cash profit at Australia's second-largest lender was A$1.80 billion ($1.26 billion) for the quarter ended June 30, compared with A$1.70 billion a year ago.
