2022-05-05T08:05:00+10:00 : May 5 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  on Thursday posted a 4.1% rise in first-half cash profit as robust growth in lending and deposits helped the country's second-biggest lender offset the impact of shrinking margins. 	NAB reported cash earnings of A$3.48 billion ($2.53 billion) for the six months to March, compared with A$3.34 billion a year earlier and Refinitiv IBES estimates of A$3.48 billion. ($1 = 1.3782 Australian dollars)
