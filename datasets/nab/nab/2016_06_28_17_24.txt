2016-06-28T17:24:00+10:00 : June 28 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB) :  Will redeem the US$600 million perpetual capital notes (capital notes) issued in 2009 by nab  Says capital notes will be redeemed on 26 September 2016,  Each capital note will be redeemed for cash at its cash redemption price  Says capital notes will be de-listed from the official list of the Luxembourg stock exchange
