2021-02-16T08:10:00+11:00 : Feb 16 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  on Tuesday said its first-quarter cash profit was flat versus a year ago, as the bank's performance was pressured by sustained record-low interest rates.	 	Australia's No. 2 lender by market value posted cash earnings of A$1.65 billion for the three months ended Dec. 31, compared with A$1.65 billion a year ago.
