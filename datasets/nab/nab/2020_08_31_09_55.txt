2020-08-31T09:55:00+10:00 : Aug 31 (Reuters) - Wealth manager IOOF Holdings Ltd  (IFL)  said on Monday it would acquire National Australia Bank Ltd's  (NAB)  wealth management business for A$1.44 billion ($1.06 billion). 	 The acquisition was expected to deliver A$150 million of pre-tax cost savings and was expected to be completed before June 30, 2021, the company said in a statement. ($1 = 1.3574 Australian dollars)
