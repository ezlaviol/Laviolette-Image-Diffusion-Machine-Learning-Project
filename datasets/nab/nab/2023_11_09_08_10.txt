2023-11-09T08:10:00+11:00 : Nov 9 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  on Thursday reported an 8.8% rise in its annual cash profit as the country's second-largest lender benefited from elevated interest rates and an increase in lending and deposits. 	The bank's cash profit came in at A$7.73 billion ($4.95 billion) for the year ended Sept. 30, compared with A$7.10 billion a year earlier. 	Analysts had expected a profit of A$7.80 billion, according to Visible Alpha estimates compiled by Citi.  ($1 = 1.5620 Australian dollars)
