2019-08-14T08:15:00+10:00 : Aug 14 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  on Wednesday reported flat third-quarter cash profit growth, and flagged that it would incur additional customer remediation-related expenses in the second half of fiscal 2019. 	Australia's No. 3 lender by market value posted cash earnings of A$1.65 billion ($1.12 billion) for the three months ended June 30, same as last year. The cash profit figure excludes one-offs and non-cash accounting items. ($1 = 1.4712 Australian dollars)
