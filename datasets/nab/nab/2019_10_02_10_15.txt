2019-10-02T10:15:00+10:00 : Oct 2 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  said on Wednesday it will incur additional charges of A$1.18 billion ($791.43 million) after tax, relating to provisions for customer remediation. 	The lender said it expects the charges to reduce its cash earnings in the second half of fiscal 2019 by around A$1.12 billion after tax, and earnings from discontinued operations by about A$57 million after tax. ($1 = 1.4910 Australian dollars)
