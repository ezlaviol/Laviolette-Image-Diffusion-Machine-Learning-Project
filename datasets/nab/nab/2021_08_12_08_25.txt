2021-08-12T08:25:00+10:00 : Aug 12 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB) , the country's No. 3 lender, said on Thursday its third-quarter cash profit jumped 10.3% as it was able to move funds set aside for potential COVID-19 losses back into profit. 	The bank said cash profit for the quarter ended June 30 was A$1.70 billion ($1.25 billion), compared with A$1.55 billion reported a year earlier. ($1 = 1.3559 Australian dollars)
