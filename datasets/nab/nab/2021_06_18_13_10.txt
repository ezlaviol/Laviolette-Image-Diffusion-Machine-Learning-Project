2021-06-18T13:10:00+10:00 : June 18 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  said on Friday it has agreed to settle a 2016 class action lawsuit in the United States over alleged manipulation of a key short-term interest rate. 	The terms of settlement in the bank bill swap rate lawsuit are confidential and without any admission of liability, Australia's third largest bank said. (https://bit.ly/35yyemo)
