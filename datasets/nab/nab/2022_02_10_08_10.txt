2022-02-10T08:10:00+11:00 : Feb 10 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank  (NAB)  on Thursday reported a 9.1% rise in first-quarter cash profit as the country's second-biggest lender benefited from growth in home and business lending. 	NAB posted cash earnings of A$1.80 billion ($1.29 billion) for the quarter ended Dec. 31, compared with A$1.65 billion a year earlier and a Morgan Stanley estimate of about A$1.60 billion.	  ($1 = 1.3926 Australian dollars)
