2021-05-26T09:25:00+10:00 : May 26 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  on Wednesday said issues related to underpayment of staff for part time work had impacted 3,200 current and former employees. 	The country's third-largest bank also noted a local media report https://www.theaustralian.com.au/business/financial-services/nab-facing-fsu-federal-court-action-over-huge-underpayments-claim/news-story/69e967b99a1a75a84efd7019d536803a  that said the Finance Sector Union was looking to lodge Federal Court proceedings against the bank for alleged underpayment of staff.
