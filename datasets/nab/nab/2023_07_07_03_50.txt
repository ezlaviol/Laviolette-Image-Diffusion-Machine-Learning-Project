2023-07-07T03:50:00+10:00 : ZURICH, July 6 (Reuters) - UBS's  UBSG.S  flagship global wealth management business on Thursday announced a raft of management changes triggered by the Swiss bank's takeover of Credit Suisse. 	Several former Credit Suisse managers have been taken on in  the changes which affect roughly 180 executives and mainly take effect from July 17.
