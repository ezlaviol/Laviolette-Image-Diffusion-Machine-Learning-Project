2020-08-14T08:15:00+10:00 : Aug 14 (Reuters) - National Australia Bank Ltd  (NAB)  on Friday reported a 6.1% drop in third-quarter cash profit as it set aside more money to cover potential loan defaults brought on by the coronavirus crisis. 	The country's third-biggest lender said cash profit came in at A$1.55 billion ($1.11 billion) for the quarter ended June 30, down from A$1.65 billion as reported last year, but above a Citi estimate of A$1.26 billion. ($1 = 1.3992 Australian dollars)
